Welcome to La Beaute Box

Hi, I'm Emma. Thank you for visiting La Beaute Box. I am a fully qualified beauty therapist with over 12 years experience within the industry. I have a special interest in nails and makeup. I am passionate about the health of the natural nail. I love to develop my knowledge and keep up to date with the latest trends and training courses. I have completed training with Bio Sculpture and Elim. I chose these products because of their proven ability to strengthen the nails and improve the condition of hands and feet. My salon is located in the village of Dullingham near Newmarket, Cambridgeshire.

Terms & Conditions

Cancellations

Labeautebox runs on an appointment only basis. When a client cancels at the last minute or misses an appointment, this costs the salon financially. Understandably, reasons to cancel an appointment can occur. If this does happen, you are required to notify the salon as soon as possible to rearrange your appointment. If less than 48 hrs notice is given, you will be charged 50% of the total cost of the treatment.

Treatment Aftercare

Please follow the aftercare given for each treatment to ensure you achieve the best results possible.

Personal Data

At Labeautebox we collect your email address to be used for booking purposes and any appointment reminders. You will also receive salon news and offers however you can unsubscribe at any time. Labeautebox will not sell or forward your information. Any information stored is kept strictly confidential and in line with GDPR. By subscribing to the emails, you are agreeing to our terms and conditions, and have agreed for Labeautebox to store your information securely.

Client consultation forms

All new clients will be requested to complete a client consultation form before each treatment. Failure to do so will result in the treatment not being performed. Please advise Labeautebox of any health conditions, allergies, or injuries that could affect your treatment when completing the form. It is your responsibility to let Labeautebox know of any changes before each treatment.

Patch Testing

Patch testing is required at least 48 hrs before certain treatments. You will be notified when booking a treatment if a patch test is required. Failure to complete your patch test will result in your appointment being cancelled.

Age Restrictions

You must be 18 years of age or over to receive any treatment. If not, parental consent will be required and a Parent/Guardian will also be required to attend and stay for the duration of your treatment.
