Welcome to La Beaute Box

Emma is a talented beauty therapist with over 12 years experience within the industry. Emma has a special interest in nails and is passionate about the health of the natural nail. The salon is located in the village of Dullingham near Newmarket, Cambridgeshire.Emma ensures she continues to develop her knowledge and keeps up to date with the latest trends and training courses.
Recently Emma completed training with Bio Sculpture and Elim. She choose these products because of their proven ability to strengthen the nails and improve the condition of your hands and feet.

Hands Treatment

La Beaute box offers a range of nail treatments from a basic file & paint to a tailor-made manicure where your nails are assessed, treated & tidied finished with Gel polish or nail varnish. The combination of preparation, application and aftercare produces long lasting results.
