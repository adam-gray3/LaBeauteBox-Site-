What is a preview?

This is where I will learn all about you & your special day. So that I can create the look that you will love. I recommend having your preview a minimum of 6 months before your wedding day. It is best to do this once you have decided on your dress and have an idea on how you would like your hair to look too. Please let me know any ideas you have & bring along any pictures you might have, so we can get an idea of the look we are working towards. I am also available anytime to answer any questions or concerns you might have. I look forward to hearing from you
